About Me

These experiences, combined with personal projects like my WiMusic API (Node.js, TypeScript, Docker), have shaped my professional identity as a developer who values both technical quality and user experience.

I am particularly interested in full-stack web development and game development. I currently work with Unity and plan to explore Unreal Engine. Beyond coding, I draw inspiration from cinema, music, and comics, which nurture my creativity and curiosity.
